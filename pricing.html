Solution options, scope, price, and support are connected. If the fixed bid does not create the right payback profile, we can adjust scope, phase the work, or revisit the solution option before delivery begins.

Up-front work

Enough discovery to make the proposal useful.

We are willing to do a practical amount of up-front discovery to understand the problem, outline solution options, and provide a fixed-price recommendation. Larger or less-defined projects may begin with a focused discovery engagement before full implementation pricing is finalized.

What affects price

The price depends on more than the build.

  • The measurable impact of the problem.
  • The complexity of the workflow, data, and systems involved.
  • The clarity of the desired outcome.
  • The level of documentation, training, and adoption support needed.
  • The amount of maintenance or iteration expected after launch.

Simple expectation

Predictable pricing before the work begins.

Once scope is agreed, the project has a fixed bid, clear assumptions, and clear success criteria. If new needs emerge, we discuss the business impact and decide whether to adjust scope, phase the work, or add support after launch.
