The point

We do fixed-bid custom pricing, not hourly billing.

Every project is scoped around the business problem, the value of solving it, and the work required to deliver a usable result. Once scope is agreed, you get a fixed price, clear assumptions, and a defined outcome before work begins.

Problem + Scope + Value = Fixed price
Why it helps

Fixed-bid pricing reflects how consulting works now: AI changes delivery economics, and customers should get financial predictability instead of a running meter.

What this changes

Pricing becomes part of the business case.

The conversation starts with the problem, the expected impact, and the result worth paying for. From there, we shape a scope and fixed bid that make sense for the value being created.

What affects price

The price follows scope and business value.

  • The measurable impact of the problem.
  • The complexity of the workflow, data, and systems involved.
  • The clarity of the desired outcome.
  • The support, training, or maintenance needed after launch.
